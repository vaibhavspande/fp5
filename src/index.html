Fascinating Facts About Blockchain .

Pizza Purchase with Bitcoin

The first ever item to be bought using blockchain was a pizza, purchased for 10,000 bitcoins in 2010. At today's price, those bitcoins would be worth over $570 million.

Blockchain in Fine Art Authentication

Is being used to verify the authenticity of fine art pieces. By creating a digital certificate of authenticity stored on a blockchain, buyers can ensure the provenance of the artwork they're purchasing..

NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens)

NFTs is a way to buy, sell, and own digital assets such as art, collectibles, and virtual real estate using blockchain technology. One notable example is the sale of an NFT artwork by digital artist Beeple for $69 million in 2021.
